my friends

This project is my idea for training in ReactJS. It is built similarly to Facebook. As anonymous user you can: publish statuses (thoughts, questions), comment yours and others statuses, get random color of user icon. As logged in user you can also like yours and others statuses, edit your statuses and comments.

scrabble

This is a small game created for children who start reading and like ciphers. Just look at the displayed letters and make a word out of them. You can choose Polish or English words by clicking on the top corner flag. Be careful, only one answer is correct ;)

stopwatch

Basic stopwatch. Counts down the required number of seconds. You can stop the elapsed time and resume the countdown. And finally covers the page with confetti.
